Restaurant Business Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI):
------------------------------------------------------
Project Overview
The restaurant/hotel management team needs a Business Intelligence solution to track and analyze sales performance and customer orders. Since the data is available in raw format, it becomes difficult to understand total revenue, total orders, category-wise sales, top-selling menu items, and hotel-wise performance.
This project focuses on building an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides meaningful insights for decision-making and helps improve overall business performance.

 Project Objectives:
 -------------------
 
Monitor Total Revenue, Total Orders, Total Customers.
Analyze Sales by Category (Veg / Non-Veg).
Compare Revenue by Hotel.
Identify Top Selling Menu Items.
Track Orders Trend by Date.

Tools & Technologies Used:
--------------------------

SQL - Data Base and Querys
Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization
Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

 Key KPIs (Dashboard Metrics):
 -----------------------------

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Total Revenue by Hotel
-  Sales by Category
- Quantity by Category
- Top Sales Menu Items
- Total Orders by Date
